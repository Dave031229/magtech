Company Profile

Electronics Magnetics: Molded & Assembled Solutions · Plating Excellence

Founded in 2024, MAGTECH Electronics Inc. is dedicated to offering a wide array of electronic component solutions and services. The company excels in advanced electroplating techniques and engineering solutions tailored for precision components.

Alongside its expertise in both high- and low-frequency magnetic components, MAGTECH provides automated inspection testing and specialized component packaging. Emphasizing innovation, the company is also focused on developing cutting-edge inductor designs, performing electronic device repairs, and supplying critical electronic consumables, establishing itself as a trusted partner in the electronics sector.

MagTech is a technology-driven manufacturer specializing in:

Low-high inductance magnetics

Molded & assembled inductors

Nickel & tin plating solutions

Automated and AI-driven manufacturing
